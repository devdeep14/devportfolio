Cleopatra: The Last Queen of Egypt

High Concept

Cleopatra came to power at the early age of 18 after the death of her beloved father, but the power struggle never ended till her death. Ptolemy XIII, her younger brother undercut her for the throne, & she had to flee to Syria to save her life. After a year, she marshaled forces and fought Ptolemy's army at Pelusium with the help of Julius Caesar of Rome to regain power. It was mutually beneficial for both, since Caesar wanted to return to power in Rome. Ultimately, they were successful, and soon they became lovers. Caesar returned to Rome, where he was assassinated in 44 B.C. that set off another power struggle. Soon, Mark Antony and Octavian took over as rulers of Rome. It was here that Cleopatra and Mark Antony entered a relationship and agreed to protect her and keep her in power. The game will be an action-adventure with mind blowing puzzles and riddles on the backdrop of Egyptian pyramids where the game proceeds to unknown treasure in hidden chambers inside the structure.
